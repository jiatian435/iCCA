Model Interpretation & Clinical Suggestion

These risk stratifications and survival estimates are predicted by the SSVM_OS (11 variables) and SSVM_DFS (9 variables) explainable machine learning models for intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma (iCCA) patients after curative resection. Risk scores are standardized using training set reference values (0-1 range), and risk groups are defined by X-tile optimal cutoffs.

Clinical Note: This prediction is an adjunct to clinical judgment, not a definitive prognosis. Treatment decisions should combine with clinical symptoms, imaging and pathological results.
